## General Behavioral
1. You ask: Tell me about a time you handled a difficult stakeholder.
Follow-ups: How did you prepare? What was the outcome?
Strong answer: Detailed specific situation with a positive resolution.
Weak answer: Vague or blaming others.
Red flag: No clear example or unprofessional language.
⸻
2. You ask: Why are you interested in this role?
Follow-ups: What specifically about our stack?
Strong answer: Clear alignment with company values and tech stack.
Weak answer: Only mentions salary.
Red flag: Knows nothing about the company.

## Leadership Skills
1. You ask: How do you handle conflict within your team?
Follow-ups: Can you give a recent example?
Strong answer: Calm, empathetic approach focusing on resolution.
Weak answer: Avoiding conflict entirely.
Red flag: Aggressive or dismissive behavior.
⸻
2. You ask: Describe your mentoring style.
Follow-ups: Who was the last person you mentored?
Strong answer: Empowering and focused on long-term growth.
Weak answer: Micromanagement.
Red flag: "I don't have time to mentor."

## Frontend Coding Challenge
1. You ask: Implement a debounce function in JavaScript.
Answer: Detailed explanation of closures and timers.
Follow-up: How would you handle arguments?
⸻
2. You ask: Explain the difference between useMemo and useCallback.
Answer: useMemo returns a memoized value, useCallback returns a memoized function.
Follow-up: When would using these be an anti-pattern?
